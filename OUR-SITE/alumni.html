CASA: High School Acting Studio graduates have gotten into:

Ball State University, Belmont, Boston College, Boston Conservatory, Boston University, Butler University, CCM, Cleveland Institute of Music, Columbia College, Columbia University, DePaul, Elon, Emerson, Florida State University, Fordham, IU, Ithaca, NYU, NKU, Roosevelt, Temple University, and Trinity,

AND most of them on scholarship!

CASA: High School Acting Studio students and private students of Gina Cerimele-Mechley have also:

  • Been accepted to summer performing arts programs in Paris, Italy, NYC, Boston, L.A., Chicago, Michigan and Carnegie Mellon
  • Been 50%-75% of the finalists in the Overtures Competition, Musical Theatre/Theatre Category for 8 years
  • Won Overtures for the past 8 years
  • Gotten paid professional work in theatre and film throughout the tri-state area as high school students

GRADUATES are currently WORKING as performers:

  • In theatre, film, and TV in L.A. and Chicago

  • On National Tours And Children’s Theatre National Tours
  • On stage at several Regional Theatres across the country
  • On Disney Cruise Lines
